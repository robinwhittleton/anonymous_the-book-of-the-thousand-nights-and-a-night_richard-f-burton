Shahrazad and Shahryar6070

King Shahryar marvelled at this history6071 and said, “By Allah, verily, injustice slayeth its folk!”6072 And he was edified by that wherewith Shahrazad bespoke him and sought help of Allah the Most High. Then said he to her, “Tell me another of thy tales, O Shahrazad; supply me with a pleasant story and this shall be the completion of the story-telling.” Shahrazad replied, “With love and gladness! It hath reached me, O auspicious King, that a man once declared to his mates, I will set forth to you a means of security against annoy.
